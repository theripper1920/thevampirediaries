About:

The Vampire Diaries is an American Supernatural teen television show which aired on The CW.It is based on the books written by L.J. Smith with the same name as the show.Its about a love triangle between two brothers and a girl.History repeats itself as the brothers turned into vampires and met a girl with the same body as the girl in their past who was burnt in the church fire.The story revolves around a town named Mystic Falls situated in Virginia founded by some families later known as the founding families.

The 1st epsiode "Pilot" aired on 10th September,2009 and aired around 171 episodes until The CW announced the last season of the series and it aired its last episode on 10th March,2017.The "Pilot" episode attracted a large audience and the 1st season had an average 3.6 million viewers.The show was nominated for various awards and won four People's Choice Award and many Teen's Choice Award
